Ages across Italian Regions

Italy's census provides very descriptive counts of their population, and counts year of age, not just age range. This isn't really helpful in trying to draw correlations with the current dataset displayed below, but if all ages were included, it could be more interesting. By including ages of all people in the country, you could see birth rate trends and life expectancy trends more easily. Even with this limited data, it can be seen that the age of Italy skews higher than 25 for example. Might also be useful for parents, seeing where there are many kids the same age as theirs, so that they can find populated schools. Or on the other end of the spectrum, retirees finding communities of people the same age as them.

Sources: TopoJSON from Deldersveld's TopoJSON Collection and ISTAT: Istituto Nazionale di Statistica
